Derek J. Paredes, Esq.

Derek J. Paredes is a partner at AAA Legal. Mr. Paredes graduated cum laude from the University of Florida in 2009 with a B.A. in Political Science and he obtained his J.D. from Florida International University College of Law in 2014. Upon becoming a member of the Florida Bar, Mr. Paredes worked as an associate attorney for a law firm in Aventura, FL where he represented various medical providers against insurance carriers in lawsuits for unpaid PIP and medical payment benefits; obtaining invaluable and extensive experience in litigating PIP cases from inception to resolution.
