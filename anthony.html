Anthony S. Fye, Esq.

Anthony Fye is a partner at AAA Legal. Mr. Fye graduated from Adrian College with a B.A. in Criminal Justice and he obtained his J.D. from the University of Toledo College of Law in 2015. While in law school, Mr. Fye gained civil litigation experience while working at the University of Toledo's Domestic Violence Clinic and at Bay Area Legal Services Elder Law Department. After passing the 2015 Florida Bar Exam, Mr. Fye worked as an associate attorney for a law firm in Tampa, FL.
